Centrelink is the Australian government department that controls welfare payments. Welfare payments are government aid for people experiencing financial difficulties, usually in the case of unemployment. When someone becomes unemployed, they contact Centrelink. Then, they will supply a small income to the individual, but after they get a job, some forget to tell Centrelink and still receive the money. It is someone's job to find overpaid people and investigate the matter by contacting the person's employer. If they are, the investigator will file a bill; this works but is slow and costly. Therefore, Centrelink came up with the idea to automate the process. The automation process was named 'Robodebt'.
